🧠Capstone Project

MediDesk: A smart hospital management system

MediDesk is a smart hospital management system designed to streamline healthcare operations through three dedicated portals: admin, doctor, and patient. The admin portal manages overall system activities, including staff, appointments, and records. The doctor portal allows physicians to access patient histories, update diagnoses, and manage schedules efficiently. The patient portal enables users to book appointments, view prescriptions, and track their medical records. Together, these integrated portals enhance communication, efficiency, and quality of care in modern healthcare environments.
